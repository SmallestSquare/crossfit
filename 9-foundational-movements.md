# 9 Foundational Movements

**Source:** CrossFit Level 1 Training Guide & Official YouTube Playlist  
**Playlist:** [9 Foundational Movements - English](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLdWvFCOAvyr0q99QIkLBq4tfYhTVbsBIs)

| Exercise/Movement          | Range of Motion (ROM)                          | Points of Performance                                                                 | Muscle Groups Activated                  | Stimulus                                      | YouTube Video |
|----------------------------|------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|---------------|
| The Air Squat             | Full hip/knee extension → hips below knees    | Lumbar curve maintained; weight on heels; knees track toes; initiate hips back; full extension at top | Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core stabilizers, erectors | Midline stabilization; posterior chain; core-to-extremity power | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rMvwVtlqjTE) |
| The Front Squat           | Full hip/knee extension → hips below knees    | Same as air squat + front-rack position maintained; bar over mid-foot; elbows high   | Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core, upper back | Front-rack loading; leg drive; thoracic extension | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m4ytaCJZpl0) |
| The Overhead Squat        | Full hip/knee extension → hips below knees    | Same as air squat + overhead position active; bar over mid-foot; shoulders pushed up | Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core, shoulders, traps | Overhead stability; full-body coordination; thoracic mobility | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RD_vUnqwqqI) |
| The Shoulder Press        | Full arm extension; neutral spine             | Lumbar curve maintained; heels down; bar close to frontal plane; active shoulders at lockout | Deltoids, triceps, upper chest, core, traps | Strict overhead pressing; shoulder stability; vertical force | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xe19t2_6yis) |
| The Push Press            | Full hip/knee/arm extension                   | Same as shoulder press + torso vertical in dip; hips extend before arms; fast turnaround | Legs, glutes, deltoids, triceps, core   | Dip-drive power; leg-initiated overhead force | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X6-DMh-t4nQ) |
| The Push Jerk             | Full hip/knee/arm extension; receive in partial squat | Same as push press + full hip extension in drive; locked arms on receive; sound partial squat | Legs, glutes, deltoids, triceps, core   | Explosive hip drive; receiving under bar; athletic power | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V-hKuAfWNUw) |
| The Deadlift              | Full hip/knee extension; neutral spine        | Lumbar curve maintained; weight on heels; bar close to shins; hips drive; full extension at top | Posterior chain (hamstrings, glutes, erectors), quads, grip, core | Hip hinge; posterior chain strength; safe loading | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=op9kVnSso6Q) |
| The Sumo Deadlift High Pull | Full hip/knee extension; high pull to chest   | Same as deadlift + wide stance; elbows high & outside; aggressive hip extension      | Posterior chain, quads, traps, deltoids, core | Explosive triple extension; high pull power | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o6QniJ9FaGA) |
| The Medicine-Ball Clean   | Full hip/knee/arm extension; ball to rack     | Same as deadlift + hip drive to shrug; elbows high & fast; ball lands in rack position | Posterior chain, quads, shoulders, traps, core | Explosive hip drive; receiving in rack; power clean entry | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-nk0GqeSTJs) |

*Data compiled from CrossFit L1/L2 Training Guides (primary points of performance) and official seminar demonstration videos.*
