# 9 Foundational Movements

**Source:** CrossFit Level 1 Training Guide & Official YouTube Playlist  
**Playlist:** [9 Foundational Movements - English](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLdWvFCOAvyr0q99QIkLBq4tfYhTVbsBIs)

| # | Exercise/Movement | Range of Motion (ROM) | Points of Performance | Muscle Groups Activated | Stimulus | YouTube Video |
|---|-------------------|-----------------------|-----------------------|-------------------------|----------|---------------|
| 1 | The Air Squat | Full hip/knee extension → hips below knees | Lumbar curve maintained; weight on heels; knees track toes; initiate hips back; full extension at top | Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core stabilizers, erectors | Midline stabilization; posterior chain; core-to-extremity power | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rMvwVtlqjTE) |
| 2 | The Front Squat | Full hip/knee extension → hips below knees | Same as air squat + front-rack position maintained; bar over mid-foot; elbows high | Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core, upper back | Front-rack loading; leg drive; thoracic extension | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uYumuL_G_V0) |
| 3 | The Overhead Squat | Full hip/knee extension → hips below knees | Same as air squat + overhead position active; bar over mid-foot; shoulders pushed up | Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core, shoulders, traps | Overhead stability; full-body coordination; thoracic mobility | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pn8mqlG0nkE) |
| 4 | The Shoulder Press | Full arm extension; neutral spine | Lumbar curve maintained; heels down; bar close to frontal plane; active shoulders at lockout | Deltoids, triceps, upper chest, core, traps | Strict overhead pressing; shoulder stability; vertical force | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5yWaNOvgFCM) |
| 5 | The Push Press | Full hip/knee/arm extension | Same as shoulder press + torso vertical in dip; hips extend before arms; fast turnaround | Legs, glutes, deltoids, triceps, core | Dip-drive power; leg-initiated overhead force | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iaBVSJm78ko) |
| 6 | The Push Jerk | Full hip/knee/arm extension; receive in partial squat | Same as push press + full hip extension in drive; locked arms on receive; sound partial squat | Legs, glutes, deltoids, triceps, core | Explosive hip drive; receiving under bar; athletic power | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VrHNJXoSyXw) |
| 7 | The Deadlift | Full hip/knee extension; neutral spine | Lumbar curve maintained; weight on heels; bar close to shins; hips drive; full extension at top | Posterior chain (hamstrings, glutes, erectors), quads, grip, core | Hip hinge; posterior chain strength; safe loading | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ZXobu7JvvE) |
| 8 | The Sumo Deadlift High Pull | Full hip/knee extension; high pull to chest | Same as deadlift + wide stance; elbows high & outside; aggressive hip extension | Posterior chain, quads, traps, deltoids, core | Explosive triple extension; high pull power | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gh55vVlwlQg) |
| 9 | The Medicine-Ball Clean | Full hip/knee/arm extension; ball to rack | Same as deadlift + hip drive to shrug; elbows high & fast; ball lands in rack position | Posterior chain, quads, shoulders, traps, core | Explosive hip drive; receiving in rack; power clean entry | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KVGhkHSrDJo) |
| 10 | The Medicine Ball Progression | Progressive med-ball drills leading to medicine-ball clean (deadlift → shrug → high pull → rack) | Ball close to body; lumbar curve; hip drive before arms; elbows high and fast in rack; full hip extension | Posterior chain, quads, core, shoulders, arms | Skill progression toward med-ball clean / Olympic lift teaching | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TlneBvU4XFY) |
| 11 | The Sumo Deadlift High-Pull Progression | Progressive drills leading to sumo deadlift high pull (setup → deadlift → shrug → high pull) | Wide sumo stance; lumbar curve; hips open before arms pull; elbows high and outside; bar close to body | Posterior chain, quads, traps, deltoids, core | Skill progression toward SDHP; triple-extension teaching | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7RwkiIjaFj4) |
