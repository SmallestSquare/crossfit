## 📌 Important Notes

- **Scaling Rule:** Always adjust volume, load, or movement to hit the intended **stimulus** (time/reps/pace).
- **Run Substitutions:** Row, Assault/Echo/C2 bike distances provided on original page.
- **Ruck Work:** Use 14/20 lb vest/backpack for skill days – build discomfort tolerance.
- **Week 6 = Deload** → Focus on recovery and mobility.
- **Murph Strategy:** Keep runs < 10:00 each. Use small, unbroken sets for bodyweight movements.
- **Advanced Option:** Add 14/20 lb vest on select high-volume days if you’ve previously completed RXD Murph.
